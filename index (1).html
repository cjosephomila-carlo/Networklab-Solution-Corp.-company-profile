About Us

Engineering the invisible backbone behind your operations.

NetworkLab Solutions Corp. specializes in extra low voltage (ELV) equipment system design and system integration. We deliver state-of-the-art design, supply, installation, and support to meet advanced information and technology requirements.

We are committed to implementing the latest technology and industry practices — providing the solutions our clients need to succeed in today's market, while maintaining a safe and healthy working environment for both clients and the employees who make it all possible.

November 2018

NETLAB is formally established as NetworkLab Computer Solution (Single Proprietorship).

January 2023

Becomes a corporation under the legal name NetworkLab Solutions Corp., expanding into Security & Surveillance, PA Systems, PABX, BMS, Data Centers, and Network Infrastructure Solutions.

Mission

To become a leading provider of innovative and integrated service solutions, giving excellence in Supply, Deployment, Design, Installation, Commissioning, and ongoing Services.

Vision

To provide clients with a highly satisfactory service experience through the best quality and latest technology in the market — beyond expectation, on schedule, and within a safe and productive workplace.
